TEDxREVAUniversity



TEDxREVAUniversity 2019 was a really great event which featured 10 speakers, 3 performances and lots of fun and motivation.

It was based on fables, which are short stories that feature inanimate objects and forces of nature that are brought to life and that illustrates or leads to a particular moral lesson and takes us through a lifelike journey, portraying good and evil and we absorb an understanding of importance of right and wrong.

TEDxREVAUniversity 2019 was the first ever TEDx event in REVA University and surely many more are going to follow on it and it is already on it's way with the announcement of TEDxREVAUniversity 2020.















Speakers


Believers. Achievers. TED speakers are the essence of a TED event. From sharing experiences to reforming young minds, TED speakers play a vital role in any TED event. Presenters can be parts of billion dollar companies, or even emerging small-scale artists. They are motivated and driven to make a change, significant or not. Realising potential and making it a part of their lives. They share what it is like to be themselves, and their challenges and solutions. With speakers of the highest diversity, TEDxREVAUniversity aims to deliver most inspiring, powerful speeches of the century.





























About


TEDxREVAUniversity is an independently organised TED event. This event is an collective endeavour of students from different schools of REVA University. The aim of this event is to share great stories and diversified ideas with our community of 10,000+ students, faculties, and staff in order to inspire them to innovate and bring the change they want to see in the world. The event will take place in the newly renovated, state of the art, Kuvempu Auditorium in REVA University, Bengaluru. The event will feature a number of speakers from various disciplines and cultures, who will narrate their stories and share their ideas with people who seek a deeper understanding of the world. We believe and promote TED’s mission of 'spreading ideas’ and their agenda to make great ideas accessible and spark conversations. TEDxREVAUniversity will present ideas which are powerful enough to change attitudes, lives and, ultimately, the world.
